Summary: Latitude vs X

The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. To accomplish this analisys, we datase on over 5000 cities.

After assembling the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot various aspects of the weather vs. Lattitude. Factors we looked at included: temperature, cloudinesss, wind speed and humidity.This site provide the source data and visualizations created as part of the analisys, as well as explanantions and descriptions of any trend and correlations witnessed.
